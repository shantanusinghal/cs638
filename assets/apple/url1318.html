The Original Is Better, However....
     
by Jeezy Jr.

this is still a great song. This is the second single released off of The Re-Up after "You Don't Know". This is a great joking song, and should be mentioned with Just Lose It, My Name Is, and The Real Slim Shady as Eminem's best joking songs. It is really creative of Eminem to use the children's song Jimmy Crack Corn as a rap song. It is songs like this that show why Eminem is the best rapper, it displays his wide versatility. He can either make a great joking song that will make you laugh, such as this, or he can make a really serious song that displays great messages. This is a great song, however, as stated in the title, the original with 50 Cent is better. Since this is the remix, it basically comes down to Ca$his's verse vs. 50 Cent's verse. Ca$his's verse is ok, with nothing special, but 50 Cent's verse carries on the great jokiness of this song and blows Ca$his's out off the water. P.S I can't wait to see what Eminem does with the music video to this.

Genre: Hip-Hop/Rap

To call Eminem hip-hop's Elvis is correct to a degree, but it's largely inaccurate. Certainly, Eminem was the first white rapper since the Beastie Boys to garner both sales and critical respect, but his impact exceeded this confining distinction.
