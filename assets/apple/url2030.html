by PhillyBreezy

While this is just the audio, this entire concert was dull and predictable. I support Beyonce's music- but when I saw this show I was a little put off. Singing one line of Deja Vu then moving into Crazy In Love, with the same routine she's done since 2003- is boring. Half of Ego was sung while she showed rehersal footage (really) and Video Phone was not sung at all. She just danced to the prerecorded song. Irreplaceable was the same it was during The Beyonce Experience. She left out songs like Sweet Dreams, Ring The Alarm and Deja Vu in favor of lesser known songs At Last, Hello and Ave Maria. And would someone please tell Beyonce that we are sick of seeing her perform Destiny's Child songs?
