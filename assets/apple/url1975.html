Customer Reviews

QUEEN BEY!!!
     
by baddieeey

Yet again I am not surprised Bey had outdid herself again. Beyoncé self-titled was phenomenal and you'd think "what possibly could she do better?" and she does it! She just opened another closet for all the wigs snatched! I love you Bey! 🍋🍋 Lemonde is my mood for 2016!

FIRST REVIEW!
     
by GeauxTOGirl

The fact that Beyoncé made this album, her best album, as a complete ode to Black women inspires me. Many will say this album is for everyone and everyone CAN indeed indulge, but a Louisiana girl like me seeing my home state and our slavery era traditions and the strength of Black womanhood on display in such powerful ways is something I'll never forget nor take for granted. Every song tells a story and Beyoncé tells it beautifully. 5 stars.

Freedom of expression!
     
by Reyskins

The level of creative freedom that Beyoncé has reached is simply awesome! Her blend of visual and musical is reminiscent of the late greats Michael Jackson and Prince! She's sharing her immense gifts with the world with no apology. She's not asking for permission to do it her way......she's just doing it. And this Beyoncé...."The Artist" is doing this on a level very few have reached! I applaud her....and thank her for the encouragement for self expression that so many will be inspired by.
