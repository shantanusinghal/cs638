iTunes Review

This EP has the additional material from the deluxe edition: two new tracks (“7/11” and “Ring Off”), live performance footage from the year-long “Mrs. Carter Show World Tour,” and new remixes featuring brilliant guest appearances by Nicki Minaj ("Flawless Remix”), Kanye West and Jay Z ("Drunk in Love Remix”), Pharrell Williams ("Blow Remix”), and Mr. Vegas (“Standing On the Sun Remix”).
