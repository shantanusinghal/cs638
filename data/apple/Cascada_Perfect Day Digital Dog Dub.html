Not so "perfect"
     
by AmarieOneLove

I'm not happy with this release by Cascada. It's the same sweeping vocals as every other song. Granted, that's what got the records famous in the first place but originality is still needed for club anthems. While I believe that the clubs will eat this up like they do with every other Cascada release, I won't be staying in the club just to make sure I catch that hot hook, because in this song, there is no hot hook. That said, if some major producers get behind this and a real remix ep comes out I may give it a second listen. However, I am still not convinced that it will be worth even that.

Biography

Formed: 2004 in Bonn, Germany

Genre: Dance

Years Active: '00s, '10s

Originally called Cascade until legal conflicts forced them to change their name, Cascada is the slick, uplifting Euro-dance trio featuring producers DJ Manian and Yanou along with vocalist Natalie Horler, although Horler is sometimes presented as Cascada herself. Horler was born in Bonn, Germany, to English parents and grew up loving all sorts of music.
