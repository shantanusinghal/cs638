iTunes Review

The considerable success of the spring 2014 release of Ingrid Michaelson’s Lights Out led to this November 2014 deluxe version, which adds six bonus tracks. Three songs—“My Darling,” “(I’ll Be Glad When You’re Dead) You Rascal You,” “When I Go”—appeared on the album's vinyl edition. Two are piano-and-voice demos of album tracks: “Over You” and “Everybody Is Gonna Love Me Now.” A previously unreleased acoustic live version of Bon Iver’s “Skinny Love”—a song she’s been performing in concert for years—completes the package. Anyone transfixed by the original album will equally appreciate these tracks.

Biography

Born: December 8, 1979 in Staten Island, NY

Genre: Pop

Years Active: '00s, '10s

With her piano-fueled songwriting, witty wordplay, and slight vocal vibrato, Ingrid Michaelson carried the tradition of the female singer/songwriter into the 21st century. Befitting a musician of the digital age, Michaelson first gained wide exposure not through sales but instead through tracks on soundtracks, including Grey's Anatomy and One Tree Hill.
