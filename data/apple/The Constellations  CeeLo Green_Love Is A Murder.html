Biography

Formed: Atlanta, GA

Genre: Alternative

Years Active: '00s, '10s

Led by vocalist Elijah Jones, the Constellations specialize in eclectic, rhythmic electro-rock that recalls the sounds of Beck, Gnarls Barkley, and the Heavy. The Atlanta-based collective features a rotating lineup of local pop, hip-hop, and rock musicians, several of whom -- including Cee-Lo and rapper Asher Roth -- contributed to the band’s debut album, Southern Gothic. With Jones calling the shots and Grammy-winning producer Ben H. Allen behind the boards, Southern Gothic was recorded in 2008...
