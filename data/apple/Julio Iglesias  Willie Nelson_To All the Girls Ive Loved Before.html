Born: September 23, 1943 in Madrid, Spain

Genre: Latin Pop

Years Active: '60s, '70s, '80s, '90s, '00s, '10s

Blessed with good looks, a powerful voice, and a gift for communicating with an audience, Julio Iglesias is one of the most popular and enduring figures in the history of Latin music, a true international superstar who has sold over 300 million albums worldwide and remains a popular concert draw and chart-topping recording artist in a career that has spanned six decades.
