Customer Reviews

Good mix of new and old
     
by MillertimeXRU

A lot of classic songs on this album by Toby Keith, Willie Nelson, Ted Nugent, and David Allan Coe. Then there are some fun new songs like Off the HIllbilly Hook and Let's Get Trashed. Then there is Rodney Carrington's song (Show Them To Me), that if you do not laugh at - then you do not have a sense of humor. It is a good album because it is unlike anything else out there, a good mixture of a lot of different country sounds.

individual song downloads limited
     
by HaHaHaHaNo

The songs are great-except for the fact that "Beer for my horses" and "if that ain't country" are only available if you buy the whole album. Three songs that are individually downloadable just barely cut it with me.
