Bridge school was a movement/event for a great cause
     
by hellitunes

I was fortunate enough to attend most of the Bridge School shows. First off the shows are truly for a good cause not one of those made up causes that get the promoter rich. These shows are and were for the kids. During the show the kids from the school get to sit up on the stage, how cool is that ! I think each performer does the gig out of the kindness of their hearts. The show is a true gift of humanity to some great kids. One of my best show memories is a halloween show when the kids from school wore some great costumes to the show. Best performances would have to go to of course Neil Young, Tom Petty, and CSN, Great performance I would like to get in the future are The Eels, Alanis Morreset, Marliyn Manson.  These shows are some of the best I have ever seen in my life and the recordings are fantastic great job all. This is a must have for any music collection.
