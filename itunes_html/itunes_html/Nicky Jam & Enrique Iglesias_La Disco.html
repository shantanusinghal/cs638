Customer Reviews

Okay...
     
by Gringo Guillermo

Much better than any compilation entitled "Reggaeton Hits". I give this a three because it is not bad, but it's not great either. Don Chezina has finally done something that I, as a Reggaeton fan, can appreciate (and I'm sure other fans could, too). Once again... it's just okay.

El Fury de Reggaeton!!
     
by Bluntside

This album is a total mix of the greatest reggaeton artists. What more could you ask for in reggaeton? All of them in a mixed album, This is it! Eso Es la Primavera. Still the best vari album out there!!
