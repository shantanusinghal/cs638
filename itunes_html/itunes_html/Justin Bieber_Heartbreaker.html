Editors’ Notes

Featuring five new tracks, a string of R&B-infused singles, a new video (“All That Matters”) and a trailer for his second feature-length film (Believe), this collection chronicles the creative energy of a prolific, multi-talented pop superstar. Highlights include “Confident”, the R. Kelly collaboration “PYD” and funk-influenced “Roller Coaster”.
