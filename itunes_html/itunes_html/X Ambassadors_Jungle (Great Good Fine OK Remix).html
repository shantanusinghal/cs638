Biography

Formed: 2010 in New York, NY [Brooklyn]

Genre: Alternative

Years Active: '10s

Brooklyn-based alt-rock ensemble X Ambassadors make passionate, dark, and literate music that mixes acoustic, electric, and electronic sounds. Originally, the band formed while singer/songwriter Sam Harris, his keyboardist brother Casey Harris, and childhood friend/guitarist Noah Feldshuh were in college.
