Biography

Born: September 19, 1986 in Asheville, NC

Genre: Country

Years Active: '10s

North Carolina-raised country singer and songwriter Chase Rice grew up listening to Garth Brooks and the other singers who reshuffled and updated country music into something closer in line with pop in the 1990s. During high school and colleger, however, he put much more of his time and energy into sports, playing linebacker on the University of North Carolina football team.
