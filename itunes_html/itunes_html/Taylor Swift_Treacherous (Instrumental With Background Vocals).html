Customer Reviews

Not totally instrumental
     
by Claudio C.C.

I know the description states there are background vocals but it's like having the original songs. The background voices are throughout the whole song and it takes the emotion of instrumental versions. "22" is one of the best songs on the album and I can't really appreciate the "instrumental version" as the vocals are really similar to the regular version.

It'd be a lot better without background vocals.

Perfect.

Taylor's music has always been extremely well written and this album is no different, but now it's the fans turns to sing lead on this Karaoke album. It's so good :) So very perfect.

Wonderful

Born: December 13, 1989 in Wyomissing, PA

Genre: Pop

Taylor Swift is that rarest of pop phenomenona: a superstar who managed to completely cross over from country to the mainstream. Other singers performed similar moves — notably, Dolly Parton and Willie Nelson both became enduring mainstream icons based on their '70s work — but Swift shed her country roots like they were a second skin; it was a necessary molting to reveal she was perhaps the sharpest, savviest, populist singer/songwriter of her generation, one who could harness the zeitgeist...
