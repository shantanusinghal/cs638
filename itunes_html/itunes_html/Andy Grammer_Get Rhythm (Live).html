by Coolpandakid97

I have been a Johnny cash fan ever since I was a little girl. Most people wouldnt think a 15 year old girl like me would even know who Johnny cash was!! I don't see how anybody couldn't know who he is considering he's a legend!! Every time I hear hurt I cry so much. It's hard to believe that that was his last song. It was as if he was saying sorry for all the bad things I've done and goodbye. Even though he has passed he's music will always and forever live on. I love you Johnny. R.I.P <3

Cash Live
     
by Jimi Rhoads

A great collection of legends and newcomers celebrate the music of Johnny Cash. This recordings do the man is black justice ten fold.

Great Idea, Poorly Executed
     
by FlyingFaders

Ultimately, it's what "makes it to tape" that matters, and this is one of the poorest quality recordings you'll find connected to such a musical legend. What's even sadder is the folks who contributed their talents to this recording end up being represented very poorly. Evidently, this was a hard project to pull off in the way it should have been done and someone decided to settle for 2nd or 3rd best. Really is a shame this didn't come off better.
