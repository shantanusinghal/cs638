by Ronny Daniels

This album is full of songs that touch your soul:) just a great way to get lost in this whole other world while listening to these songs!
I was lucky and got to watch the movie during the Catalina Island film festival which was great! The movie was so well directed and shot!
Glad to see that there are still some great people filming and making these kinds of movies and music!

Rudderless
