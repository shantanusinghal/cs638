Disappointing...
     
by DJ Beatdaddy

Aside from Maya Jane Coles & FC Kahuna, mostly forgettable stuff here. Sad cause this used to be the go to release for winter chill out.

Maybe forgettable.. But not bad
     
by lilitalia

Great music to play in the background. Who cares if it's forgettable? That's how I prefer my chill music to be.. Chill in the moment and not memorable enough that I can't get it out of my head the moment it's off.

Winter got warmer
     
by Drizzy Mac

Listening to this on Sundays is bliss. Perfect morning after music. This is best played while recovering from Saturday night, played throughout the dorm, apartment or duplex while going over what you think happened last night. Amazed. Love Hed Kandi, hope they keep making classic music. Stay blessed.
