Album Review

Having already written hits for Miley Cyrus and Maggie Rose, singer/songwriter Cam (aka Cam Ochs) breaks out on her own with this four-song EP. It's a short introduction, and maybe even a bit rushed, but if getting the clever hit "My Mistake" ("But he's my mistake to make all night") was the impetus for the EP, so be it. The highlight and earworm is surrounded by equally infectious numbers, with "Half Broke Heart" being a close runner-up.
