by Vincymck

I have to call BS on VP Music Group.
How much longer are you going to keep recycling old music with the addition of a few new songs and call it a new album?
For someone that bought all of the earlier series of Strictly the Best this is getting annoying. You keep putting on old music from your earlier series as a ploy to increase the album count and charging more money for it.
For me to buy the album I am paying for tunes that I already own from your earlier series and if I only buy individual songs; which is more expensive than buying the album; I am now forced to buy the album to get all of the new sounds.
This practice started from Strictly the Best 46 and I refuse to buy any more “Strictly the Best” albums after that. I think this is a big scam that is being pushed by VP and I for one have had enough. I will be getting my reggae from other distributers.
