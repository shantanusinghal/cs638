Big ups for the Martin Solveig song.
     
by Well, wouldn't you like to know.

I am really excited to see "The Night Out (Madeon Remix)" by Martin Solveig on here. It's been out for a while and it has become one of my ultimate favorite songs to workout to because the intensity really pushes me when I workout. I'm a little surprised, however, that the song is on this mix because all the other songs don't have as much intensity as this song does, although the other songs are great as well. It's quite a jump in intensity from the transition from "Catch My Breath" to this song, but the song works well in this mix and I really love the album as a whole! I bought the first one and it really pushed me at the gym, so I was really looking forward to another one, seeing as the NOW series consistently puts out more volumes of each of their different compilation types. I highly recommend buying this whole album, but if you only want one song, then go buy "The Night Out (Madeon Remix)" on Martin Solveig's page.
