Customer Reviews

Nice Release!
     
by LuvDance

For those of you who don't know anything about dance music remixes, there's a market for us who want to listen to them. U + Ur Hand just hit #1 on the Billboard Dance Airplay chart, and it is because fans like us like that remix. A weakness with this release is that there should have been a radio edit for U + Ur Hand Bimbo Jones edit. I personally like 12" version, but my friends prefer the radio edit.

Remixes are very inportant
     
by inkahootz

I can't believe that someone would slash Pink for this release. We should be thankful these mixes are available for download, considering the terrible state the music industry is in and how difficult it will become to get tracks such as these in the very near future.

U + UR Hand is worth it alone!!!!
     
by SigningBear

This is the Remix I've been waiting for Itunes to get (You think you can get the Dave Aude mix of "Buttons" and the Lenny B. Mix of "We Ride" too?). The Bimbo Jones Remix is a killer on the Dance floor and gets every one Dancing to it. I like the other Remixes as well on here but not as killer as U+UR Hand Remix.
