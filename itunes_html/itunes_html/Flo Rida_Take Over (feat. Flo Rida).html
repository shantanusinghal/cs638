Biography

Born: Ireland

Genre: Dance

Years Active: '90s, '00s

An Irish DJ whose first big break came with a remix gig for the Source, Micky Modelle was best known for his work with Belgian vocalist Jessy on their hit single "Dancing in the Dark" in 2006. Modelle got his start at the age of 15, DJing parties and such, but it was his work in the clubs that got him signed to Intelligence Records.
