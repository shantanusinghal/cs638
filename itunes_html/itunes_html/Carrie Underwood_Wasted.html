Editors’ Notes

This greatest-hits collection is a signal that Underwood's not done yet; new song "Something in the Water" became a smash single before the album was released. Classics like "Before He Cheats" and "Jesus Take the Wheel" are joined by unreleased demos, a new track "Little Toy Guns," and the tune that started it all: "Inside Your Heaven.”
