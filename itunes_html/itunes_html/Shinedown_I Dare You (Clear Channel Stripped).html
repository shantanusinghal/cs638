First Review, Great Album-Decent Bonus Content
     
by yankeefan774

This is a outstanding album by an exceptional band that didn't get as much popularity as their most recent one, The Sound of Madness, but is still worth the listen. Along with the thirteen original songs, the deluxe version offers plently of bonus content including some live, acoustic, and demo songs. I am a big fan of the acoustic versions but the otherstuff no so much. The live versions are crowded out with backround noise and come across and sloppy. The demos are alright but sound very similar to the final recording. This is a must have great album but i reccomend just getting the regular version.

Biography

Formed: Jacksonville, FL

Genre: Rock

Years Active: '00s, '10s

Melodic hard rockers Shinedown hail from Jacksonville, Florida and originally featured vocalist Brent Smith, guitarist Jasin Todd, bassist Brad Stewart, and drummer Barry Kerch. Snapped up by Atlantic Records during the early-2000s flurry of post-Creed and Nickelback signings, the group released its debut album, Leave a Whisper, in 2003.
