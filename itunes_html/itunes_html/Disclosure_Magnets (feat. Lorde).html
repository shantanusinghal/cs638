Editors’ Notes

Disclosure may have began as a house music duo, mimicking the vintage sounds of late '80s Chicago dance floors, but they've long since found their true calling: bringing those distinct club vibes to the pop/R&B world. Caracal is the culmination of all their experiments, where soulful pop tunes featuring The Weeknd, Sam Smith, and Lorde are all underpinned by Disclosure's peerless, propulsive sense of rhythm. There are sizzling pads, swelling basslines, and huge spatial textures—but also danceable hooks, making Caracal as much an achievement of songwriting as it is production.
