Editors’ Notes

With Selena Gomez having grown out of her teen idol phase, the endlessly buoyant For You represents the closing of a chapter of the young star's career, collecting her biggest hits from the last five years. The set includes the breakout "Tell Me Something I Don't Know" and "Love You Like a Love Song," among other highlights from her three albums with The Scene, as well as the monster hit "Come & Get It" from her solo debut. Gomez recorded two new songs for the project: "Do It" and the provocative "The Heart Wants What It Wants," about her relationship with Justin Bieber.
