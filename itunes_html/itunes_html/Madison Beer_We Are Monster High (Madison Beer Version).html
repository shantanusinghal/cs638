Biography

Born: March 5, 1999 in Jericho, NY

Genre: Pop

Years Active: '10s

Madison Beer is a pop singer and actress from Long Island, New York known for her effervescence. A music lover and experimenter from an early age, Beer started posting YouTube videos of herself singing covers of varied well-known songs as a 13-year-old in 2012. That summer, pop idol Justin Bieber came across her version of Etta James' "At Last" and shared it with his over 25 million Twitter followers.
