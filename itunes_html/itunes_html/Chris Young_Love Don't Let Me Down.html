Editors’ Notes

Gwyneth Paltrow as a Nashville country music star may seem like a hard sell but just listen to the opening title track to the 2010 movie Country Strong, where she nails a convincing drawl and holds down soaring choruses without the need for Auto-Tune effects. Chris Young and Patty Loveless follow her with the endearing little duet “Love Don’t Let Me Down” before Sara Evans delivers the emotional “A Little Bit Stronger.”
