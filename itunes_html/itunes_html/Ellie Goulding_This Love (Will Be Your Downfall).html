Editors’ Notes

Lights overflows with pop that’s as infectious as it is intelligent. The rich organic resonance of a wooden acoustic guitar changes the tone on “Guns and Horses” while keeping Goulding’s sprightly energy intact. “Starry Eyed” is a standout tune that blends helium-infused balladry with tasteful Euro-dance.

Customer Reviews

A Vocal, Lyrical, and Musical Princess from the UK
     
by juxterjinx

Ellie Goulding's album has been out for quite some time in the UK and has been holding a firm spot in the top albums since it was released over a year ago. The album has since seen a deluxe version release named 'Bright Lights'.

Ellie's voice has an ethereal and honest quality to it and is so refreshing in the age of auto-tune. But Ellie is by no means a vocal instrument that just sings what is put in front of her like many artists today. In fact, she wrote or co-wrote lyrics for all of her songs (obvious exception here being Your Song' by Sir Elton John)!

This, of course, could be a very unimportant thing or even a bad thing -- Mariah Carey has taken on a lot of her song writing as of late and we see how that's gone -- but the lyrics are powerful and honest (much like her voice). The tracks 'This Love (Will Be Your Downfall)'; 'Under the Sheets' and 'Guns and Horses' deal with some complex feelings and relationships.

Now, as a singer-songwriter Ellie could easily have gone down the organic, acoustic route and done splendidly, but instead she chose to collaborate with Starsmith (primarily), Biffco, Fraser T Smith, and Frankmusik who tend to edge on the electronic side of pop. This could have gone very wrong, but somehow these producers managed to elegantly capture Ellie's messages and translate them into masterpieces.

The American version includes most of the good tracks (painfully missing is 'Human' and 'Home' from Bright Lights and 'I Wish I Stayed') and is something I would recommend for truly anyone -- no matter if you're a fan of Adele, Lady Gaga, or Bruno Mars, this album is difficult not to like.

I'm very excited for Ellie's future endeavors and hope she does well here in the States!

Biography

Born: December 30, 1986 in Hereford, England

Genre: Pop

Years Active: '00s, '10s

Having caught the public's attention in 2010 with her singles "Starry Eyed" and "Guns and Horses," British vocalist Ellie Goulding quickly rose to the top of the charts with her infectious electronic pop sound. Although she started out writing songs in a folk-inflected style, she eventually grew into a more electronic-influenced artist, touching upon dance music and ambient synth pop.
