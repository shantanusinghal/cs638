Shinedown Rocks!
     
by cbd89

I have been in love with Shinedown for about a year now ever since I went to see them open for 3 Doors Down in Columbus, GA. Since then I have been to another concert where they performed with Seether in Atlanta, GA. Shinedown kicks some major *** live and everyone needs to hear them in person just once. Before you buy this buy Leave a Whisper and Us and Them if you havn't already done so. They both are awesome CDs. To get to this EP, the first two songs came from Shinedown's Us and Them special edition CD. I love this two songs. Brent has an amazing voice and it really shows on these two tracks. "Break" comes from an Us and Them exclusive that was either at Best Buy or Circuit City. I really love this song and I don't know why they didn't put it on the album. The "Save Me" remix I heard somewhere on the net that some guys put this together and the Shinedown guys loved it. When some people heard that there was a "Save Me" remix, they were not enthusiastic about it. When they heard it though they loved it. In short, buy this EP and support the best band ever!!

Biography

Formed: Jacksonville, FL

Genre: Rock

Years Active: '00s, '10s

Melodic hard rockers Shinedown hail from Jacksonville, Florida and originally featured vocalist Brent Smith, guitarist Jasin Todd, bassist Brad Stewart, and drummer Barry Kerch. Snapped up by Atlantic Records during the early-2000s flurry of post-Creed and Nickelback signings, the group released its debut album, Leave a Whisper, in 2003.
