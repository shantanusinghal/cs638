Customer Reviews

Just better than the movie more or less
     
by 16 HORSES

We were all lead to believe this would be a big time movie like Jurassic Park or Titanic,but not to be! But the soundtrack to Snakes on a Plane was very much better than the movie! The Hush Sound And Cee Lo Green were great songs for the most part! Play a drinking game where you do a shot every time someone mentions Snakes and Planes and when you hear dialogue from the movie on this soundtrack! You're trying to promote your movie I get that, But that's a little much! Outside that I recommend the soundtrack!

Still a Fun Album
     
by Glitch-y

I never saw the movie, but some of these songs were playing everywhere back in 2006. So, on a whim I bought the album and friggin' loved it. I still listen to it while driving or studying in graduate school.

I definitely recommend this album for anyone looking to add a fun mix to their studying, their drive, or workout at the gym.
