This album will NOT disappoint!
     
by TheNovelust

Ed Sheeran's performance at the Roundhouse at this summer's ITunes Festival was yet another opportunity for him to showcase his talents for the world. No matter how great he may sound once a producer has gotten through with one of his studio albums, Ed always manages to turn out an even more incredible live performance, even if you're experiencing it from the comfort of your home. His interaction with the audience, the way he was able to stay fired up and flawless throughout the entire hour's set just goes to show what great musicianship he has, and how grateful he is for everything he's been given as he shoots to fame. A talented performer instrumentally and vocally, this album will NOT disappoint!
