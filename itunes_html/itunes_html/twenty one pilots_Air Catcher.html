This is good stuff.
     
by CDI_

After listening to the majority of this album on their MySpace page, I finally bought it, and I can't express how happy I am I did that. Every song is powerful enough on it's own to be a single. And the entire album shows a band that is composed of strong rhythms; melodic, catchy, and thought provoking vocals; keyboards that make you wish you could play along; and finally a bass line that is perfect in every way.

All in all this is one of the rare albums I have found where I like every song, some I admit are better than others, but I hope to see this band go places, it will be a great shame if they never do.

Biography

Formed: 2009 in Columbus, OH

Genre: Alternative

Years Active: '10s

Twenty One Pilots were formed in 2009 by high school friends Tyler Joseph, Nick Thomas, and Chris Salih. The pop trio built an enormous regional across Ohio and the Midwest, spurred on by constant energetic touring, shrewd use of interactive social media, and connecting with fans on a grassroots level.
