Biography

Born: December 14, 1992 in Wildomar, CA

Genre: Pop

Years Active: '00s, '10s

Singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist Tori Kelly was a would-be child star who grew into a woman who learned how to follow her own muse and bring her music to her fans through the Internet. Victoria Loren Kelly was born in Wildomar, California on December 14, 1992. Born into a musical household — her father sang and played bass, her mother was proficient on keyboards and saxophone — Kelly grew up listening to gospel and was influenced by pop and R&B acts such as Jeff Buckley,...
