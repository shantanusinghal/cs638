Some great, some okay
     
by Remixed Feelings

Another solid remix package from Gaga. I highly recommend the DJ White Shadow remixes, especially the Trap Remix, which will make your speakers shake for sure. My other favorites are Purity Ring (cool electronic mix) and the Empire of the Sun remix (his a light disco/house vibe, lots of fun). The Goldhouse remix is also worth mentioning. The Bent Collective is nice if you're looking for a traditional, by the numbers club anthem.
