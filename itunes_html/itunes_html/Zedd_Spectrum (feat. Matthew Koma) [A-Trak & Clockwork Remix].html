E-P-I-C-!
     
by Anthony Iboy

Zedd have made a masterpiece of a song here. I am in love with Matthew Koma's vocals.

Each producer on board bought their "A-Game" in here.
Trance legends Armin Van Buuren & Arty stepped out of their usual styles and created 2 gorgeous pieces of art.
House kings A-Trak, Clockwork, & Congorock (the former 2 as a team-up) have banged it all out, sending out euphoric moments, one after another.
Dirty dutch masters Deniz Koyu, Gregori Klosman, & Tristan Gartner (the latter 2 as a team-up) have hitted the bass controls like crazy, making people want to bobble their heads until the very end.
Dubstep darling Monsta dropped a MASSIVE nuclear bomb that is going to make people crank up the volume to the max!

Also, the acoustic version is beautiful (make sure that there's a box of Kleenex to dry those tears out of your eyes), & the bonus collaboration "Human (w/ Nicky Romero)" is an amazing little treat.

In terms, no words can describe how I feel about this EP.

Except one: WOW.

Biography

Born: September 2, 1989 in Kaiserslautern, Germany

Genre: Dance

Years Active: '10s

Born Anton Zaslavski, German producer Zedd broke onto the European electronic scene in 2010 with a remix of Skrillex's "Scary Monsters and Nice Sprites." Though known for his glitch, pulsing production, Zedd's musical journey began in the acoustic realm. Born into a musical family, the future producer cut his teeth on the piano at four years old and eventually moved on to drums and played in a rock band before being drawn into the digital world in 2009. Zedd built a name for himself with his remixes...
