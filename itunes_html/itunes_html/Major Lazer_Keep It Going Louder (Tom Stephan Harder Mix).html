Biography

Born: 1970 in New York

Genre: Dance

Years Active: '90s, '00s

Progressive house DJ Tom Stephan was raised in the upstate New York town of Olean but moved to London to go to college. It was in London that he started his DJ career, opening up for Danny Tenaglia and eventually hosting regular house nights at the Turnmills club.
