Editors’ Notes

“Ode to Sleep” opens with distorted synthesizers buzzing over boomy beats and menacing bass lines. When the song suddenly shapeshifts into a catchy electro-tinged pop song, we get a glimpse of this duo’s brilliance and penchant for taking risks. “Holding on to You” downshifts into a relaxed groove while maintaining Joseph’s dual personality of hyper rhyme-wizard and dynamic pop crooner.

Biography

Formed: 2009 in Columbus, OH

Genre: Alternative

Years Active: '10s

Twenty One Pilots were formed in 2009 by high school friends Tyler Joseph, Nick Thomas, and Chris Salih. The pop trio built an enormous regional across Ohio and the Midwest, spurred on by constant energetic touring, shrewd use of interactive social media, and connecting with fans on a grassroots level.
