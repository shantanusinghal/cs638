Customer Reviews

Even Better
     
by Antonio Iboy

I'm impressed here. I thought that Ultra Records would never redeem themselves from the trainwreck disaster of Ultra Weekend 6, but they learned their mistakes for the better. The song selections are nice, and the transition to the songs were great, even when the Skrillex remix of Cinema was up next. I am satisfied with my purchase, no complaints needed. Keep on coming like this, Ultra, and you'll do fine on your comeback!
