Biography

Formed: 2010

Genre: Hip-Hop/Rap

Years Active: '10s

An R&B and hip-hop collective, Diddy - Dirty Money was the brainchild of Sean "Puffy" Combs, aka Diddy. Looking to create something he categorized as "future soul," Diddy recruited singer and former Danity Kane member Dawn Richard, along with singer/songwriter Kalenna Harper.
