Customer Reviews

Truly Blown Away
     
by ZOMBIE BUTTS

I don't think I've ever experienced so many emotions while to listening to just one album. Carrie Underwood's latest album, Blown Away, literally has blown me away. Early on in the album, we get a feel of some of the darkest numbers in Underwood's entire career. "Blown Away" depicts a story of a girl who wants her sin filled house and her abusive father to be destroyed by violent tornados. In "Two Black Cadillacs", the listeners get in on a dark secret that two women share about a man's death. One of them is the late man's wife, and the other his mistress. We get a lighter feel when the sweet twang of the guitars in "Do You Think About Me" start in, and a tale of how young and genuine love becomes only a memory as time goes by is told. Underwood's raw vocals and emotion brings the song to life, and you can just see the bittersweet scenes as the song goes on. "Forever Changed", a tender story of a woman's happy life that ends in the tragedy of alzheimer's, easily brings tears to the listener's eye. "Good In Goodbye" does not trail far behind when it comes to the same effect. More emotions are sparked with "Thank God For Hometowns". It reminds the listener of the truths regarding their origins. The three chords in the beginning of "Wine After Whiskey" are enough to enthrall any listener. After all the heaviness of the powerful and emotional numbers, moods brighten with the catchy chorus of "Leave Love Alone" and the pun-tastic "Cupid's Got a Shotgun". Carrie Underwood has matured as an artist and has released an album that will be timeless in no time.

I am sincerely BLOWN AWAY.
     
by smittylc23

Blown Away, Carrie Underwood’s fourth album, is by far her best album to date. Up until this album, her debut album, Some Hearts, has always been my favorite. I felt as if Carnival Ride and Play On were rushed and that they were overproduced. In Blown Away, her voice really shined through and the songs are absolutely incredible.

We all know that Carrie can BELT songs out. For instance, I Know You Wont off of Carnival Ride is a masterpiece, and her rendition of How Great Thou Art. But on this album, there are no power ballads. But sometimes, less is more. This is one of those times. LESS is MORE on Blown Away. There are not many albums where I thoroughly enjoy each and every song on the album. This is an exception. I love each song.

9/10 - Good Girl - This is a really rockin’ country hit. It’s lyrics are so catchy and will instantly get stuck in your head. Very difficult song to sing because of all of the higher notes in it, but Carrie does it flawlessly.

10/10 - Blown Away – This song is a masterpiece. I don’t know what else I can say. It is absolutely, positively amazing.

11/10 – Two Black Cadillacs – a very deep and dark song. It really leaves it up to your imagination as to what really happened. VERY ADDICTING SONG. Carrie’s really upped her game in writing songs. One of my favorite songs on the album.

9/10 – See You Again – it was originally written by Carrie for the Narnia soundtrack, but they chose There’s A Place For Us instead…. And I am SO glad! THIS SONG IS AWESOME! Love it! Very soothing, inspirational, and soaring!

9.5/10 – Do You Think About Me – wow. I just love this song. Such a cute, sweet, and loving song! So so so so SO cute!

8.5/10 – Forever Changed – this is a song that is near and dear to Carries heart. It is about a woman going through life, getting married, having a baby, then growing old and dealing with Alzheimers. Very sad. Very well written and sung beautifully.

7.5/10 – Nobody Ever Told You – a very inspirational, uplifting song. This one is probably my least favorite song off of the album. It’s cute though.

10/10 – One Way Ticket – Carrie said that when recording this song, she brought in margaritas so that every one would loosen up. You can definitely tell that they were loose! LOVE THIS SONG! Such a summer get out of this town kind of song! Carrie is definitely feelin goooooood ☺

9/10 – Thank God For Hometowns – love this song! For anyone that moved away – you will love this! Such a sweet song.

9.5/10 – Good In Goodbye – love this song as well. A good song about breaking up and moving on. Very positive message.

8.5/10 - Leave Love Alone – so country! So awesome! Definitely a foot tapper!

9.8/10 - Cupid’s Got a Shotgun – LOVE THIS! One of Carries most creative songs yet! SO redneck and SO fun to listen and jam out to!

10/10 – Wine After Whiskey – MUST BE A SINGLE! Such an amazing ballad. Very country, sweet, reminiscing song about lost love.

7.6/10 – Who Are You – this song is growing on me. A song about Carrie’s faith in her savior. Wasn’t too fond of it at first, but like I said, its growing on me!

Maybe I’m being modest, but I don’t think I am. This album is a MUST BUY. It is GREAMMY worthy!
