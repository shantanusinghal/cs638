Album Review

For his 13th solo album, Ashes & Fire, the prolific singer/songwriter Ryan Adams recruited legendary producer Glyn Johns, who helmed the Who classic Who’s Next. The 11-track collection also includes guest appearances from Norah Jones and Tom Petty & the Heartbreakers’ keyboard player Benmont Tench., Rovi
