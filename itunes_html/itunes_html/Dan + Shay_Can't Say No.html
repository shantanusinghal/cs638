Editors’ Notes

The country-pop duo of Dan Smyers and Shay Mooney don’t bother striking faux-rebel poses when celebrating the joys of falling in love and keeping the fire alive. Their debut, Where It All Began, is an unabashedly romantic set filled with nostalgic vignettes (“19 You + Me,” “First Time Feeling”) and steamy backroads snapshots (“Somewhere Only We Know,” “Parking Brake”). The music is honeyed yet muscular, accented by hip-hop vocal moves, banjo licks, and bursts of rock guitar. Dan and Shay bind it all together with impeccable harmonies that especially shine on ballads like “I Heard Goodbye” and “Close Your Eyes.”

Biography

Formed: December, 2012 in Nashville, TN

Genre: Country

Years Active: '10s

Delivering a bright, polished sound that owes as much to contemporary pop as anything in the classic country handbook, Dan + Shay is the country singing and songwriting duo of Dan Smyers and Shay Mooney. Both began playing music as teenagers, with Smyers (born and raised in Wexford, Pennsylvania) discovering songwriting at the age of 14 then learning the guitar at the age of 16, and Mooney (born and raised in Natural Dam, Arkansas) began writing and playing music at the age of 12.
