Customer Reviews

FUTURE BASS DOESN'T MEAN DUBSTEP...
     
by Jam Master JJ

Title says it all.
This is an excellent collection of new school garage, dark house and a few trap tunes.
Chances are, if you only listen to artists like Skrillex, you won't like this!
This is a bit more emotional and deep and exudes feeling. Good stuff.

Ugh
     
by whotheowl

The kids that are ranking this 1 star are the ones who give 5 to Skillex and Tiesto... This is a great compilation from MOS on some great up and coming sounds.. Some progressive techno, a little trap... It's music for the intelligent festival goer. Be sure to check out Maya Jane and tnght

Great Music!
     
by Lana Huston

It's really nice to have bass complication that has stuff outside of the brostep subgenre. Don't bother with the 1 stars, it's good music!
