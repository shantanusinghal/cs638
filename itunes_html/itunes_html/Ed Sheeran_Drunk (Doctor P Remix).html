Customer Reviews

Excellent Representation of UK Electronica
     
by Jam Master JJ

A lot of US people aren't really aware of what's going on in the UK dance scene other than dubstep… This compilation pulls from several genres and is a BEAUTIFUL epitome of GOOD UK Dance! Not just your popsy, mainstream Skrillex, but your not well known but excellent artists like Culprate, Culture Shock, Foreign Beggars, Dillon Francis etc.
I am super excited to see a DJ Marky & SPY track… I love me some chill d&B!
Overall, I am VERY impressed with the representation and variety of music included with this compilation. Technically, your buying 40 songs, each one 25 cents! AND YOU GET 2 OVER 1 HOUR CONTINUOUS MIXES… DUDE
I recommend this album to anyone new OR old to the Dance scene! Anyone who is a d&b/electro house/dubstep head would benefit from buying this compilation!
