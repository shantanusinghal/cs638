Album Review

A country vocal quartet devoted to harmony and multiple lead vocals, Little Big Town found a home on the Dixie Chicks' label, Monument Records. This set packages four of the group's albums, 2002's self-titled Little Big Town, 2005's breakthrough The Road to Here, 2007's A Place to Land, and 2010's The Reason Why, in a single package.

Biography

Formed: 1998 in Nashville, TN

Genre: Country

Years Active: '90s, '00s, '10s

The country vocal quartet Little Big Town began with Kimberly Roads and Karen Fairchild, two Georgia natives who began singing together in college. Arkansas-born and Alabama-raised Jimi Westbrook, a friend of Fairchild's husband, joined them to make a trio, and the group was completed by the addition of Arkansan Phil Sweet in 1998.
