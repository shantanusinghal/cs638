Album Review

Although its members have had plenty of success co-writing hits for other country stars (Dierks Bentley, Kenny Chesney, Luke Bryan, etc.), this self-titled EP is the first proper release from Nashville country-rock quintet Old Dominion. Formed in the late 2000s, Old Dominion spent several years writing and touring before finding success with their 2014 single "Shut Me Up," which kicks off this five-track set of hooky, radio-ready songs. Led by singer/songwriter Matthew Ramsey and featuring fellow songwriters Trevor Rosen and Brad Tursi, they combine a distinctly Nashville sound with plenty of rock energy, pop melodies, and even traces of hip-hop.
