These 2 Will Be Huge
     
by So cal music guru

Get to know TJ and John Osborne. These 2 are not only amazing musicians but bring a new spin to the Country genre. A little rock, a little country, a TON OF SOUL. You'll hear amazing instrumentation on the first single here, "Lets Go There", killer harmonies, and a toe-tapping kick off to their highly-anticipated album. John ranks right up there with the guitar greats: Stevie Ray, Urban, Vince Gill. and TJ's baritone is as soulful and effortless as Amos Lee with a side of Springsteen. These 2 are what Country Music needs: not overly-produced, nor manufactured music. The REAL DEAL. First time I heard them live I was sold. Get to know these 2 brothers from Maryland. You will NOT be disappointed!

Biography

Formed: 2011 in Nashville, TN

Genre: Country

Years Active: '10s

Nashville-based duo Brothers Osborne make earthy, passionate country-rock. Raised in Deale, Maryland, siblings John and TJ Osborne grew up listening to their father's country and rock records. Encouraged to write and play their own music, the brothers began to practice in a shed behind their house. By their teens they had formed the cover band Deuce & a Quarter, playing songs by Lynyrd Skynyrd, Merle Haggard, Bob Seger, and others in local bars.
