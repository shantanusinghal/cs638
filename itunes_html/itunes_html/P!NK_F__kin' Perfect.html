Editors’ Notes

Greatest Hits…So Far!!! gathers 16 singles from P!nk’s first five albums, bookending with three previously unreleased songs. “There You Go” bubbles and pops with effervescent R&B punctuated by acoustic guitar. “Get the Party Started” is a streamlined dance-floor banger., “Don’t Let Me Get Me” displays a universal vulnerability. “Stupid Girls” cleverly rails against the Girls Gone Wild mentality.
