Customer Reviews

Good Collection
     
by Mr. Houck (Utah)

Some of these songs are a few years old, but doesn't mean this isn't a bad collection. Personally I already have almost of these songs and I think almost any EDM fan would, so if you are just getting into EDM and want some of the popular artists and some of their better songs then get this.

Superstars?
     
by reality90

How do they end up putting names on this such as Otto Knows & Zedd, but no Tiesto????? Have they ever performed for the opening ceramony at the Olympics??

Where's the editing?
     
by Teh Flub

This is practically a Now That's What I Call Dance! album, but every song has "(Superstar DJs Edit)" next to it's name, which I find quite annoying. Honestly, if you listen to Dance you should own most of these songs already, and the omission of Tiesto from the album is a mixture of surprising, confusing, and downright disappointing. If you're new to Dance I guess you could buy the album. P.S. don't tell Deadmau5 he's a DJ, he quite dislikes the label
