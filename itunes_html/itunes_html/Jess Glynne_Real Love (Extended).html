Biography

Formed: 2009 in Cambridge, England

Genre: Dance

Years Active: '10s

Rather than the usual bombast or snooze-inducing "relaxation music," the U.K. group known as Clean Bandit mash classical music and pop forms for an airy and dreamy yet driven sound that's like the chamber music version of house music. The group's four core members were known as the Chatto Quartet, a classically trained string quartet that performed J.S. Bach and cited Jacqueline du Pré as an influence, but their post-show, wind-down music often consisted of garage, 2-step, Craig David, and Arcade...
