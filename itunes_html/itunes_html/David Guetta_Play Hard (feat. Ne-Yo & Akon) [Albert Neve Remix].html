It's A Compilation Album!
     
by Mr. Houck (Utah)

With everyone saying why he did make this, well he wanted to put out a compilation album from some of the tracks that he as used from his sets! Everyone does it Tiesto, Armin, Hardwell and others I don't understand the hate. The album it's self is pretty good it does have a lot of songs that are popular like Easy and Sweet Nothing, but it also has tracks from artists that I really don't know about like Arno Cost "Head Up". It has song pretty rocking electro tunes also so if you are looking for those they are here. Overall a pretty good compilation album, wished it wasn't album only, but still good at least he doesn't have tracks from Flo Rida or Will.i.am from here!
