Biography

Born: October 20, 1989 in London, England

Genre: Pop

Years Active: '10s

The deep and soulful vocal tone of London-based Jess Glynne became widely known when streams of "My Love," her collaboration with Route 94 — the house-focused alias of dubstep producer Dream — appeared online in late 2013. The track went on to hit the U.K. number one spot in March 2014, but not before "Rather Be," featuring another Glynne guest spot — this time with the inventive chamber music-meets-house act Clean Bandit — had reached the same chart position that January....
