Editors’ Notes

Even the soundtrack to this star-crossed love story is a tearjerker. Kaleidoscopic pop troupe Grouplove set their typically ecstatic sound to smolder on "Let Me In," and even pop-hop upstart Charli XCX broods a bit on "Boom Clap." Bookended by Sheeran's plaintive "All of the Stars" and M83's soaring "Wait," this album should come with a box of tissues and a bowl of warm soup.
