Customer Reviews

So disappointed
     
by xerxes1492

First-off, it’s pretty one-dimensional, which was fine for Vol. 1 since trap was still on the come-up and was in its copycat stage. But now it’s almost 2014 and the genre is expanding. Most of the tracks are really poorly-mastered, tinny crate fodder with a bunch of people featured multiple times. Secondly, whoever compiled this forgot a laundry list of amazing producers. TNGHT and Rustie – two of the biggest names in trap – are missing as well as HudMo and Lunice by themselves. Others that got overlooked include Djemba Djemba, TWRK, and Ta-ku

YES!!

Can't wait for All Trap Music 2 to drop! Been wanting this one since All Trap Music. This is yet another great trap album!
